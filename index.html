Macli'ing Dulag

Kalinga Chief - Defender of Cordillera

Highlights of Macli'ing Dulag's life:

  • 1930 - Born in Kalinga Province, Philippines
  • Served as a porter to guerilla forces fighting against the Japanese in World War II
  • 1960s - Became a respected leader of the Butbut tribe. Elected for three terms as barrio captain of Bugnay village
  • 1974 - Organized a bodong (peace council) to oppose the Chico Dam Project of then-president Ferdinand Marcos Sr.
  • April 1977 - He was one of 16 Bugnay villagers incarcerated by the PC for two months
  • June 1978 & December 1979 - Macli-ing and other opposition leaders organized two of the largest bodong councils ever
  • April 24, 1980 - He was assinated by the military forces
